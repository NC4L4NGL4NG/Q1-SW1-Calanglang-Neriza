Why I love Happy Hardcore

What IS Happy Hardcore? , Happy Hardcore is a subgenre of hardcore dance music or "hard dance". It emerged both from the UK breakbeat hardcore rave scene, and Belgian, German and Dutch hardcore techno scenes in the early 1990s.

Why do I like it?

  • Electric Beats: It makes me feel alive and daydream the beat in action in my head.
  • Nice Vocals: In all honesty, the vocals make a nice touch on it in a way.
